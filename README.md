## **VanillaPlus**

### *The Ultimate Survival Enhancement Toolkit*

**VanillaPlus** is a lightweight, high-performance plugin designed to refine the survival experience without breaking the "Vanilla" feel. Built for **Java 21** and optimized natively for **Paper**, it bundles essential utility and anti-grief measures into one seamless package.

---

### **🚀 Key Features**

#### 🛏️ **Advanced Custom Sleep System (New!)**

Take complete control over how your server skips the night:

* **Flexible Sleep Modes:** Configure the skip to trigger based on a fixed player count or a dynamic percentage of online players.
* **Smart Time-Progression:** Seamlessly advances the day to your exact configured wake time, even during daytime thunderstorms, without messing up world statistics.
* **Global Phantom Protection:** Resets the rest timer for *every* player in the world upon a successful skip, preventing unfair phantom spawns.
* **Weather Preservation:** Toggle whether sleeping clears the sky or naturally preserves ongoing rain and storms.

#### 🛡️ **Smart Anti-Grief, Creeper & Ghast Control**

You are in total command of how mobs interact with your world:

* **100% Block Drops:** Creepers and Ghasts will drop every single block they destroy as an item—keep the danger of the blast without losing your rare materials.
* **Griefing Toggles:** Allow explosions to hurt players while keeping the terrain perfectly intact. Works for both Creepers and Ghasts!
* **Total Defusal:** Disable Creeper/Ghast explosions entirely.
* **Spawn Toggles:** Stop creepers/Phantoms from spawning altogether.
* **Enderman Protection:** Stop them from picking up blocks. Includes customizable notification messages when they try!

#### 🗼 **Enhanced & Scaling Beacons (Updated!)**

* **Dynamic Range Modifiers:** Boost your base safely! Beacon range now utilizes a scaling modifier that expands defaults naturally based on the beacon's tier level. Covers your entire kingdom without needing massive pyramid structures.

#### 🎒 **Portable Storage**

* **Virtual Backpacks:** Access extra storage anywhere in the world! This uses a clever book-based interface, meaning **no mods or resource packs** are required for your players.

#### 📊 **Dynamic Tab-List Stats**

* Keep your players informed with live statistics directly in the Tab menu. Track player deaths and the last time since they slept.

#### 👻 **Optional Horror Elements**

* Want to spice things up? Enable rare, "psychological horror" world events to keep your players on their toes. (Fully optional and toggleable!)

### **How to Install**

1. Drop the `VanillaPlus.jar` into your `plugins` folder.
2. Restart your server.
3. Edit the `config.yml` to set your custom messages and toggles.
4. Use `/vp-reload` to reload the plugin and apply changes instantly!
